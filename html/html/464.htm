基因(座)名称稻黑尾叶蝉抗性基因
Green rice leafhopper resistance 4
基因符号Grh-4;
所在染色体3 （未克隆）
供体材料

Grh4 conveys resistance to green rice leafhopper and acts complementarily with Grh2 located on chromosome 11. The two complementary genes convey higher level of resistance.

Located between two RFLP markers, Y3870L and Y3870R on chromosome 3 with the distances of 1.4 and 0.7 cM, respectively.

Grh2 and Grh4 were introduced into Norin-PL5 and Norin-PL6 from Indica type varieties, C203-1 and Lepe dumai, respectively.
